Why this view exists — the attribution problem
If you track reviews by property, a caretaker who covers two venues with a consistent weakness will appear as two independent property-level problems. You'd dispatch two separate investigations and find nothing structural to fix — because the issue is a person, not a place. This view re-attributes review signals to the responsible individual before routing. The rule: if the same guest complaint follows the same caretaker across different properties, it's a people issue. Route to Operations manager for coaching, not the property ops queue.
